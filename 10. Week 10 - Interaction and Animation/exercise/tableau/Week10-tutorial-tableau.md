# Week 10 - Visualization (Tableau)

## Goal

Add **Dashboard Actions** (Filter & Highlight).

## Instructions

### 1. Open Dashboard

1.  Go to your Dashboard (Week 6).

### 2. Use as Filter (Quick Method)

1.  Select the **Bar Chart** on the dashboard.
2.  Click the **Funnel Icon** (Use as Filter) in the top-right corner of the sheet container.
3.  **Test**: Click a bar. Watch the Scatter plot and Pie chart update to show only data from that month.

### 3. Dashboard Actions (Advanced)

1.  Go to **Dashboard > Actions**.
2.  **Add Action > Highlight**.
    *   Source Sheets: Scatter Plot.
    *   Target Sheets: All.
    *   Result: Hovering points highlights data across other views.
3.  **Add Action > Filter**.
    *   Source Sheets: Pie Chart (Time of Day).
    *   Run action on: Select.
    *   Result: Clicking "AM" filters the whole dashboard to AM runs.
