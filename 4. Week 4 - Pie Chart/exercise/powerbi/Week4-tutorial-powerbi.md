# Week 4 - Visualization (PowerBI)

## Goal

Create a **Donut Chart** showing Runs by Time of Day.

## Instructions

### 1. Get Data

1.  Load `Week 4/data/time_of_day_counts.csv`.

### 2. Create Visual

1.  Select **Donut Chart** (or Pie Chart).
2.  Drag `Category` (AM/PM) to **Legend**.
3.  Drag `Count` to **Values**.

### 3. Format

1.  **Slices**:
    *   Change colors to distinct shades (e.g., Light Purple, Dark Purple).
2.  **Detail Labels**:
    *   Content: "Category, Percent of Total".
    *   Position: Outside.
3.  **Legend**:
    *   Position: Right Center.
