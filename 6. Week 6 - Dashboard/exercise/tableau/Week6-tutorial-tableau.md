# Week 6 - Visualization (Tableau)

## Goal

Assemble a **Dashboard** from previous sheets.

## Instructions

### 1. New Dashboard

1.  Click the **New Dashboard** icon (window with a plus sign) at the bottom.
2.  **Size**: Set to **Automatic** or Fixed (e.g., 1000 x 800).

### 2. Add Sheets

1.  Drag **Sheet 1** (Bar Chart) to the canvas.
2.  Drag **Sheet 2** (Scatter Plot) to the right.
3.  Drag **Sheet 3** (Line Chart) to the bottom left.
4.  Drag **Sheet 4** (Pie Chart) to the bottom right.

### 3. Layout Objects

1.  Use **Horizontal** and **Vertical** containers for precise control.
2.  **Titles**: Right-click sheet titles to Hide or Edit.
3.  **Legends**: Removing redundant legends helps clean up the view. Click the dropdown on a legend > Remove from Dashboard.
