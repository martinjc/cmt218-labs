# Week 7 - Visualization (Tableau)

## Goal

Create **Text KPI Cards**.

## Instructions

### 1. Connect to Data

1.  Connect to `kpi_stats.csv`.

### 2. Build the View

1.  Drag `Measure Names` to **Columns** (or Filter to select specific measures).
2.  Drag `Measure Values` to **Text** (Label) on the Marks card.
3.  This creates a text table.

### 3. Format as Cards

1.  **Layout**: Fit > Entire View.
2.  **Text**: Click **Text** on Marks card > **Edit Label** (...).
    *   Make the Values **Bold** and Large (Size 24).
    *   Make the Labels (Measure Names) smaller (Size 10) and gray.
    *   Center Align.
3.  **Borders**: Add column dividers to separate the numbers visually.
