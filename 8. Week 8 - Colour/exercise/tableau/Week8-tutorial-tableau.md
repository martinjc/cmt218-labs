# Week 8 - Visualization (Tableau)

## Goal

Apply **Date-driven Color** to the Bar Chart.

## Instructions

### 1. Modify Week 1 Chart

1.  Open your Bar Chart sheet.

### 2. Apply Color

1.  Drag `TotalDistance` (the measure on Rows) to the **Color** card.
    *   This colors bars by their height.
    *   *Alternative*: To ensure it's a gradient, edit colors.

### 3. Custom Palette

1.  Clck **Color** > **Edit Colors**.
2.  Choose "Purple" (Sequential).
3.  (Optional) **Stepped Color**: Set to 5 distinct steps for a banded look.
